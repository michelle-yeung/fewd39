About Michelle

Michelle was born in Hong Kong and raised in Vancouver, Canada. She moved to San Francisco to pursue her education in Business Administration and Political Economy at the University of California, Berkeley. Michelle currently works at Salesforce in the Finance & Strategy group as a financial analyst supporting Core Products Finance.

Michelle enjoys reading, photography, exploring new restaurants, as well as travelling, in her spare time.

First thing about me is that I love to take photographs. It allows me to be creative and also capture transient moments forever.
I love to travel. It allows me to learn about new cultures, meet different people and push myself past my comfort zone. I try to go somewhere, far or close, at least once a month.
I love to learn, especially outside the classroom. Whether it's visiting a museum or reading a book, I'm a nerd-at-heart.
